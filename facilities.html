Resort Facilities

Our Facilities

Discover the amenities that make your stay unforgettable. From relaxing pools to family-friendly play areas, we've got it all.

Pools

Main Pool

A spacious 25-meter pool with lounge chairs, perfect for laps or lounging under the sun. Open daily from 7 AM to 10 PM.

Kids' Pool

A shallow, splash-friendly pool for children with fun water features. Supervised during peak hours.

Restaurants

La Bella Italia

Authentic Italian cuisine with fresh pasta and wood-fired pizzas. Dinner reservations recommended.

Asia Fusion

Exotic Asian dishes, from sushi to stir-fries. Open for lunch and dinner with a vibrant atmosphere.

Buffet Haven

All-you-can-eat international buffet for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Family favorites and dietary options available.

Children's Playground

A safe, colorful outdoor area with swings, slides, climbing structures, and shaded seating for parents. Open sunrise to sunset, with daily activities for kids aged 3-12.

Gym & Fitness Center

State-of-the-art equipment, yoga classes, and personal trainers. 24/7 access for guests.

Spa & Wellness

Relax with massages, saunas, and treatments using natural products. Book in advance for the best slots.
